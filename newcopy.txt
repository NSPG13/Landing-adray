FAQ
What is Adray?
The first AI-native marketing data system. We reconcile your Meta, Google, GA4, and Shopify data into precomputed, AI-ready snapshots — then pack them into a secure portable link you drop into any AI. No dashboard. No new tool. Just your AI chat, with data it can actually work with. Native MCP connectors for Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini — for real-time, conversational queries — arriving Q2 2026.
Who is Adray for?
Any business running digital ads — ecommerce brands, B2B companies, marketing agencies. If you’re spending on Meta or Google and want your AI to help you make better decisions, Adray works for you. It runs on any website — Shopify, WooCommerce, custom builds, anything.
How is this different from Triple Whale?
Triple Whale is a dashboard — you log in, read charts, and leave. Adray is infrastructure — we pack your data into a portable link you drop into the AI you’re already using. No new interface to learn. No monthly dashboard fee to justify. And because Adray precomputes and normalizes your data before it reaches any AI, you get better answers than you’d ever get by connecting raw platform data. Different paradigm entirely.
How is this different from Windsor.ai?
Windsor connects your ad platforms to AI — so does Adray. The difference is what happens before the AI sees your data. Windsor passes through raw platform numbers. Adray precomputes, normalizes, and reconciles them first — resolving conflicts between what Meta claims, what Google claims, and what actually happened. The result: your AI gives you answers you can act on, not just numbers to interpret. And with Adray’s portable link, your data travels with you to any AI — you’re not locked into one interface.
How does the data link work?
The moment you connect your first platform, Adray generates a secure portable snapshot of your marketing data. Paste it into any AI and ask questions in plain language. Refreshes with every sync. Native MCP connectors — one-click deep integration — arriving Q2 2026.
Do I need to install the pixel to get value?
No. Connect your ad platforms via OAuth and your data link is ready immediately. The pixel is an enrichment layer — install it and you unlock deeper attribution: reconciliation, deduplication, session-to-order stitching. But you don’t need it to start.
How does the pixel install work?
Via Google Tag Manager — Adray’s agent installs the tag for you, you click Publish. Or paste a single script directly into your site. Works on any platform. No developer required.
How fast is setup?
Under 2 minutes to your first data link. Connect Meta, Google, GA4, or Shopify via OAuth — your link is ready as soon as the first one connects.
