The Strategic Pivot
Why we are repositioning as a data layer, not a dashboard.
4

The Problem Worth Owning
Every ecommerce merchant is drowning in conflicting signals.
Adray — Internal Strategy 2026  |  Confidential
The Mixed Signals Problem
A merchant running $50k/month in ads sees three completely different stories:
Meta Ads Manager
40x ROAS
Google Ads
8x ROAS
Shopify Analytics
12 orders attributed
Nobody knows what's real. Budgets get allocated to whichever platform is most convincing that day — not what's actually driving revenue.
Why Dashboards
Don't Fix This
✕  Still rely on platform-reported data
✕  No first-party signal to reconcile against
✕  Merchants must learn a new tool
✕  Platform APIs will close the gap
✕  Race to the bottom on features
The real solution is a proprietary first-party data layer that reconciles all three platforms. That layer becomes more valuable as native connectors proliferate — not less.

SECTION 07
Risks & Mitigations
What could go wrong, and how we defend against it.
19

Risk Register
We acknowledge these risks openly. Each has a clear mitigation.
Adray — Internal Strategy 2026  |  Confidential
Shopify removes our app
Likelihood: Low    Impact: High
Mitigation:
adray.com direct path stays live. WooCommerce + BigCommerce connectors in Phase 3. Pixel data stickiness reduces urgency even if blocked temporarily.
Meta / Google build native LLM connectors
Likelihood: High    Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
They stay siloed. Their connector only sees their data. Adray reconciles across all three. We become the reconciliation layer — more valuable, not less.
Shopify builds native attribution tool
Likelihood: Medium    Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
Shopify can't see Meta or Google data natively. The mixed-signals problem is cross-platform. Our pixel + cross-platform reconciliation remains the unique value.
Competitor copies the model (free + MCP)
Likelihood: Medium    Impact: Medium
Mitigation:
First-mover advantage in Shopify app store reviews and install count matters. 12+ months of pixel data creates switching costs. Speed is our defense.
LLM platforms restrict third-party data connectors
Likelihood: Low    Impact: High
Mitigation:
MCP is open protocol — Anthropic's own standard. Direct API access remains. adray.com path is always available as backup consumption layer.
